Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies strictly for the functioning of our service.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as logging in, filling in forms, or paying.

  • Session Cookies: Used to maintain your login session so you don't have to log in on every page.
  • Security Cookies: Used to prevent Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF).

3. Marketing & Tracking

We do not use any third-party marketing, advertising, or analytical tracking cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not then work (e.g., you will not be able to log in).
