2. Data Collection and Usage

We collect and process personal data only to the extent necessary to provide a functional website and our content and services.

a. Visiting the Website

When you visit our website or application, our servers (including Python Flask console logs) may automatically record standard data provided by your web browser. This includes:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of visit
  • Pages visited

This data is used for technical troubleshooting and security purposes.

b. Registration and Account Data

When you register for Ordria (as a Supplier), we collect:

  • Email address
  • Company Name
  • Password (stored as a secure hash)

We use this data to provide the service, manage your account, and billing.

c. Order and Buyer Data

As part of the service, you (the Supplier) enter data about your orders and buyers. This may include:

  • Product details and descriptions
  • Order pricing and files
  • Buyer names or details (if entered)

Buyers accessing the order link do not need to create an account. We store the details of the order to display it to the buyer provided with the link.

d. Emails

We typically only send transactional emails, such as:

  • Password reset instructions
  • Important status updates regarding your account or orders

You can contact us via hello@ordria.com or hello@benedictjaros.com.

3. Payment Processing

We use PayPal for processing subscription payments and facilitating payments between Suppliers and Buyers. We do not store full credit card information on our servers. Payment data is handled directly by PayPal.

4. Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary for the operation of the service (e.g., to keep you logged in). We do not use third-party marketing or tracking cookies.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information. You also have the right to object to processing and the right to data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us.
